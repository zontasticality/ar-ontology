---
title: Propositional Formula
type: structure 
---

A propositional formula is a type of [[is-a:logical formula]] that only contains boolean variables and the following connectives, modifiers, and constants:
 - And $A \land B$
 - Or $A \lor B$
 - Not $\lnot A$
 - Xor $A \oplus B$
 - Implies $A \rightarrow B$
 - True $\top$
 - False $\bot$
